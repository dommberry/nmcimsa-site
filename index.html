About Us

NMC-International Model Science Academy established by National Mathematical Center is a world-class science school. The Academy has produced great scholars in Medicine, Pharmacy, Nano Chemistry, Aeronautical Engineering, Mathematics, Informatics within and outside the country.

Students are exposed to international competitions like the International Science Olympiads, PAMO-G, Pan African Mathematics Competitions, etc., conducted by the Mathematical Centre.
